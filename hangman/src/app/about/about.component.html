About

Hangman is a very simple pen-and-paper guessing game for two players. In this implementation, you are always the guessing side.

Hangman Rules

  1. A word is randomly selected from our database
  2. You are given the category of the word (for example - programming, cooking, sports, cars, etc
  3. A number of underscores equal to the length of the word is displayed.
  4. You need to choose a letter from the alphabet until
    1. The entire word is guessed
    2. You have missed six times - in this case, you lose.
